Green Tile Social Club is an
NYC-based mahjong community
that creates new spaces for the
Asian American experience to
flourish and thrive.

We believe in the power of play
to bridge generations and
connect strangers through
the click-clack of tiles and
shared laughter.

From beginners to pros,
everyone has a seat at
our table. Come shuffle
with us and find your
community.
