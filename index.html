Green Tile Social Club

Green Tile Social Club is an NYC-based mahjong community that brings Asian Americans together through our shared love for mahjong + culture.

It all started in May 2022 with four Chinese, Cantonese, and Taiwanese American friends — and one impromptu night of mahjong that led to many, many more.

These days we get to bring Chinese mahjong all over NYC — from local Asian-owned businesses to cultural institutions like the American Museum of Natural History and New York Public Libraries.
